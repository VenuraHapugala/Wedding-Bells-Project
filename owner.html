WHO WE ARE About us Owner of the company


Bryan Fernando is the chairman of the wedding Bells company.A warm smile is what it all starts with. Questioning and listening intently, Bryan gets to know each couple and company crew. He has the intuitive instinct to seeing and understanding the dream, each couple has, for their special day.

Then with boundless enthusiasm, he ensures that their day is a true reflection of who they are. It is often very difficult to tell who is more excited about the wedding –Bryan or his clients. He truly has invested in helping create the vision for the day with no stress, lot of fun, and most of all, has genuinely cared to make it a unique personalized experience.

Since the day he started Wedding Bells Company, Bryan has chosen to define success as something beyond. His vision and intuition has always filled with optimisitic hopefulness to raise the standard of the wedding events in Sri Lanka. His goal is to inspire everyone he works within the wedding bells company and overall industry across the country to reach for new levels of creativity and developments. His mission is to provide the kind of exceptional service that eliminates any undue pressu on his customers. So, they get the greatest amount of happiness and satisfaction working together from day one leading to their special wedding occasion. When the suprising day arrives,his well pre planned arrangement coordination and visualization makes clients feel more confident and assured because of the experiences they’ve had with him along the planning journey.

And when it’s all over Bryan hopes every Wedding Bells company customer will remember the great service and his ccordination done through the company as remark in their heart with happy gladness.
